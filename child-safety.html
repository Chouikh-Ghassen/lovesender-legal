LoveSender Child Safety Standards

Our commitment to child safety and protection

Last updated: March 2026

LoveSender is committed to maintaining a safe environment and has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

1. Age Requirement

LoveSender is intended for users aged 16 and older. Users must be at least 16 years old to create an account. We do not knowingly allow minors under the age of 16 to use our platform.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

LoveSender has a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content that exploits, harms, or endangers minors. This includes:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Content that sexualizes minors
  • Grooming or exploitation of minors
  • Any communication intended to harm children

3. How Our App Works

LoveSender is a private, closed communication app. Users can only connect with and send feelings to people they explicitly invite and accept. There is no public feed, no ability to discover or contact strangers, and no open chat functionality. This closed architecture significantly limits opportunities for child exploitation.

4. Reporting Mechanism

Users who encounter any content or behavior that may endanger a child can report it directly to us by email. All reports are taken seriously and will be investigated promptly.

To report a child safety concern, contact us at:

ghassenchouikh@gmail.com

5. Compliance with Authorities

LoveSender complies with all relevant child safety laws. In the event we become aware of any child sexual abuse material on our platform, we will:

  • Immediately remove the content
  • Suspend the offending account
  • Report to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) as required by law
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities

6. Account Termination

Any account found to be involved in child exploitation or abuse will be permanently terminated immediately, and the matter will be reported to the appropriate authorities.
